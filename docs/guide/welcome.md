# Welcome to Burnmark

Burnmark is for **people who print labels** — not only professional prepress operators, but anyone who has said: “I will just hand-write forty spice lids” and regretted it.

## Who we build for

- **Home & kitchen** — pantry jars, freezer bags, leftovers with dates, kids’ craft drawers.
- **Hobbies** — miniatures bins, yarn colour codes, tool chests, cable harnesses.
- **Online sellers** — **marketplace shipping** labels, SKU stickers, “fragile / this way up” cues on demand.
- **Light commercial** — a laptop, a thermal printer, and a spreadsheet — no IT department required.

## What makes it different

**Browser-first.** You are not asked to install a Windows-only driver CD from 2009. The experience assumes a modern **Chromium** browser, secure context, and Web platform APIs where hardware allows.

**Honest about hardware.** Colour on screen becomes **black, red, or dithered grey** depending on the printer family. The design engine **flattens** at export time so surprises happen in the preview, not on the roll.

**Templates with merge fields.** Save a layout once, drive it from a **CSV** for Christmas cards, club memberships, or a Monday morning pick list.

**Sheet PDFs.** When you need **Avery L7160-style** pages instead of roll stock, the **sheet-templates** data describes real-world grids — including awkward multi-layout sheets.

## For developers

Burnmark publishes **npm packages** (`designer-core`, Vue/React helpers, CLI). If you embed the engine in your own SaaS, start at [Libraries](/libraries) — this site’s “people printing at home” story still applies to how documents serialize and render.
